XD Inc. is a global video game developer and publisher with extensive experience in development, publishing, and live operations. Its portfolio includes self-developed titles such as GoGo Muffin, Torchlight: Infinite, and Heartopia, as well as published hits including Sausage Man and Dave the Diver.

As a company listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, XD currently has over 1,300 employees.

TapTap Overview

Business Model

TapTap's business model is built on a positive cycle between developers and players.

By maintaining a 0% revenue share model and no co-publishing policy, TapTap attracts quality developers and games, which bring in more players.

Growing user engagement improves advertising efficiency, generating sustainable revenue while preserving fair content distribution and supporting ecosystem growth.
